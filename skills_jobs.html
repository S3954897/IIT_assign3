Skills and Jobs

Lead Developer

Description

This role requires both leadership and App development experience, as the Lead Developer will be directly working with the Team Manager to create and deliver the product. You will be working within a Team of 5, where you’ll oversee the development process, ensuring deadlines are met and to an adequate quality approved by the Team Leader.

Appropriate Skills and Experience

The applicant will require at least 3 years of experience in App Development and provide evidence of previous leadership roles in their past employment.

  • The required skills for this role are the following
  • Proficiency with HTML, JavaScript, Python and CSS
  • Experience with any RDMS (MySQL, MSSQL, Postgres, etc) type database
  • Experience with deliver and planning a product from start to finish
  • Verbal communication, coaching and leadership skills are highly valued
  • Experience with APIs
  • Experience using code source control such as Git

Qualifications

A Degree in Computer Science or related field is required for this role. Any qualifications in leadership or team management will be favourable to your application.

User Interface and Application Developer

Description

This role requires the application to be experienced in developing applications for both IOS and Android as they will be primarily working on developing the app under the guidance of a Lead developer. You will be working within a team of 5, where you’ll be working heavily with app development and UI creating and design.

Appropriate Skills and Experience

The application will require at least 2 years experience in mobile app development experience in their previous employment.

  • Proficiency with HTML, JavaScript, Python and CSS
  • Past experience with any RDMS (MySQL, MSSQL, Postgres, etc) type database
  • Experience using code source control such as Git
  • Experience developing native mobile applications for iOS and Android
  • Experience in creating UI and UX for applications

Qualifications

A Degree in Computer Science or related field is required for this role.

Backend Developer

Description

This role requires the applicant to be experienced in managing and developing the backend infrastructure of an application. You’ll be working within a team of 5 where you’ll be heavily working within the database infrastructure for the application being developed and will be expected to work within the deadlines and under instruction of the Team Manager.

Appropriate Skills and Experience

The application will require at least two year’s experience in backend development experience in their previous employment.

  • Past experience with any RDMS (MySQL, MSSQL, Postgres, etc) type database
  • Server-side knowledge
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Visual Studio and Enterprise tools
  • Knowledge of Website Monitoring Tools and understanding the Alerts to fix the issues

Qualifications

A Degree in Computer Science or related field is required for this role.

Lead Developer

Description

This role requires the applicant to be experienced in reviewing, testing and ensuring the overall quality of the application being developed. You will be working within a team 5 where you will be working with all members of the team, testing and advising any issues in quality within the application during the development process.

Quality Assurance Engineer

The application will require at least 2 years’ experience in quality assurance experience in their previous employment.

  • Experience across a wide range of test phases (SIT, UAT, E2E, etc), tools (JIRA, Oracle, AWS, etc) , methods (i.e. functional, non-functional and automation testing)
  • Knowledge of testing with BDD, TDD, Microservices and in cloud-based environments
  • Understanding of HTTP (REST/GraphQL), databases and interface definitions
  • Past experience with any RDMS (MySQL, MSSQL, Postgres, etc) type database

Qualifications

A Degree in Computer Science or related field is required for this role.
